> about

I'm a fullstack engineer and AI systems builder based in Nairobi, Kenya. I spend most of my time at the intersection of backend architecture and AI — building autonomous workflows, RAG pipelines, and LLM-powered tools that replace manual processes.

When I'm not wiring up agent orchestration, I'm designing API-first backends that handle payments, geolocation, real-time delivery, and async job pipelines. I connect these to interfaces people actually want to use — SPAs, mobile apps, and real-time dashboards.
